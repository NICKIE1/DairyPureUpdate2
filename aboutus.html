About Us




DairyPure aims to deliver fresh milk providing you extreme convenience while serving a great purpose.
DairyPure is fully operated by a Cooperative society, hence no more involvement of middlemen!
Cooperative society is a group of dairy farmers who come together to procure and sell milk.
These cooperatives are typically owned and governed by their member-producers, and they operate to collectively market and share revenue generated. Our website enables users to order fresh milk online which will be delivered at selected time slot with no hassle!
Users can also take a subscription plan with customizations as per their convenience. Subscription option will be added soon.
DairyPure's social media platforms communicate with users, share great information about dairy products and promote the initiative.
By operating online, a dairy cooperative society can supply fresh milk to wider customer base and increase its sales, while also generating rural employment.
