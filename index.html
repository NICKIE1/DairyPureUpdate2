Our Products

Buy milk from an array of organic milk options available with us

Low fat milk

Low fat milk is made without adding anything to the milk which retains our milk's natural properties. Essentially, our Low Fat Cow Milk is not just low fat milk but also high protein and mineral milk. An excellent source of calcium and protein, and essential Vitamins like D and B12, low-fat milk in your daily diet can help fulfill your daily calorie requirement without adding fat to your diet.

Raw milk

The natural sweetness of our cow's milk is the hallmark of purity and becomes an instant favourite among kids. The milk you receive has an abundance of Vitamin D, B, B12, Calcium, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Potassium and amino acids - a combination of powerful nutrients essential for the development of your mind and body.

Toned Milk

If you are a person who loves the purest and creamiest milk from the countryside, DairyPure's Toned Milk is the right product for you! The protein-rich milk you receive every morning is sourced from Murrah Buffaloes, directly procured from our farmers. A glass of buffalo milk everyday is a wholesome source of Calcium which is crucial for bone strength. It contains Potassium that stabilizes blood pressure and boasts of other essential minerals as well.
